✦ Introduction 2026

IT Girl or It Girl : Just a girly who got curious about technology.

Hello everyone, I'm Faye an it girl and an IT girl at the same time currently studying Information Technology. This blog is where I share my experiences, the skills I have learned and the challenges I face as a student in the field of technology while also loving girly things that are not usually associated with this field.

All this time all I cared about were makeup, fashion and other girly stuffs, when technology caught my attention I became curious about how technology works in this growing digital world. That curiosity slowly turned into an interest and eventually led me to pursue Information Technology.

📖 My Journey 2026

How It All Started — My ICT Days in Senior High School

Before choosing IT as my program in college, I already started learning about it during Senior High School. I took the strand ICT (Information and Communications Technology) which is closely related to Information Technology. I chose this strand because of my curiosity as a girl who once only liked girly things that got interested in technology and also because of the influence of people around me who are the same as me. Some of them were already professionals in this field, which inspired me even more.

During my time in ICT, I learned the basics of programming and different languages used in web development including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. These were my first steps into the world of coding and technology. At first, it was challenging because programming was very different from the things I was used to. But as I continued learning and practicing I started to enjoy the process of creating and understanding how websites and systems work.

💭 Challenges 2026

The Hard Parts — Being a Second-Year IT Student

Right now, as a second-year college student I face even more challenges while learning deeper concepts in Information Technology. There are times when I question myself and wonder if I should continue especially when lessons become difficult to understand. One of the things I struggle with the most is understanding complex coding concepts of programming which requires a lot of logic, patience and continuous practice. Sometimes it takes me a while to fully understand how certain codes work or why errors happen.

"However, instead of giving up I remind myself that learning technology is a process and that every mistake helps me improve."
🌸 Written by Faye
🎨 What I Love 2026

Why Web Designing Feels Like Getting Dressed

Despite these challenges, there are also parts of my course that I truly enjoy. One of my favorite areas is web designing, because it allows me to combine creativity and technology. Since I was already interested in graphic designing during Senior High School, designing websites feels exciting and familiar to me.

In a way, creating a website feels similar to doing makeup or putting together an outfit. Just like in makeup where you carefully choose colors, blend different shades, and highlight the best features, web designing also involves selecting color palettes, arranging elements and making everything look balanced and attractive. It is also like dressing up, where every piece needs to match and work well together to create a complete look.

✨ Reflection 2026

Being Both — Girly and IT Student

Being an IT student while still loving makeup, fashion, and other girly interests makes my journey unique. It shows that technology is for everyone, regardless of their hobbies or personality. You can love coding and still enjoy being fashionable or creative.

Through this blog, I hope to share my journey as an IT girl who also embraces being an it girl. I want to show that you can follow your passion in technology while still staying true to who you are.
